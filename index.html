Habit Flow — Privacy Policy

We believe your data belongs to you.

Effective Date: April 27, 2026
Welcome to Habit Flow. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Habit Flow mobile application. By using our app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.
1 Information We Collect
1.1 Account & Identity
  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo (stored as encrypted data in our database)
  • Phone number (optional, only if you choose to provide it)
  • Gender (optional)
  • Date of birth (optional)
  • Account creation timestamp
  • Unique user identifier (Firebase UID)
1.2 Habit & App Usage Data
  • Habit names, types, colors, schedules, and configurations
  • Daily habit completion records and history
  • Streak counts and experience points (XP)
  • Mood logs and journal entries
  • Archived habits and tasks
  • Progress reports and statistics
1.3 Device & Preference Data

We store the following locally on your device only — this data is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Notification preferences and schedules
  • App appearance settings (theme, accent color, font)
  • Biometric authentication preference (processed locally, never transmitted)
  • Onboarding completion status
1.4 Purchase & Subscription Data
  • Subscription status and entitlement information
  • Purchase history and transaction records
  • Your Firebase UID is linked to RevenueCat for entitlement verification
2 How We Use Your Information
  • Create and manage your user account
  • Provide cloud backup and sync of your habit data across devices (Pro feature)
  • Send habit reminders and notifications you have configured
  • Display your progress, streaks, and statistics
  • Process and verify subscription purchases
  • Restore purchases after reinstallation
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Respond to customer support requests
We do NOT use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it for any purpose beyond operating and improving Habit Flow.
3 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), a secure cloud database. Profile photos are stored as encoded data directly in your user record. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase security rules restricting access to your own data only
  • Biometric authentication option for app access (processed on-device only)

While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5 Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers (Firebase, RevenueCat) strictly to operate the app
  • When required by law or valid legal process

6 Your Rights & Choices
6.1 Access & Correction

You can view and update your profile information at any time within the app under Settings → Personal Info.

6.2 Data Deletion

You can request account deletion at any time from within the app under Settings → Account & Security → Delete Account. Upon request, your account will be deactivated and you will be signed out.

30-day grace period: If you sign back in within 30 days, you can cancel the deletion and fully restore your account. After 30 days, the next time you attempt to sign in, all your personal data, habit history, and account information will be permanently and automatically deleted from our servers.
If you never sign in again after requesting deletion, your data will be retained until you do, or until we perform periodic manual cleanups of inactive accounts. For immediate manual deletion, contact us at keshavkatariya2004@gmail.com.
6.3 Notifications

You can disable all notifications at any time through the app's notification settings or your device's system settings.

6.4 Data Portability & Export

You can export your data at any time through Settings → Account & Security → Export My Data. Available formats:

  • JSON — full backup of all habits, history, and mood logs
  • CSV — spreadsheet-compatible flat table
  • PDF report — formatted summary with stats and streak highlights
Non-Pro users export their locally stored data. Pro users export their full cloud-synced dataset. All exports are generated on your device and shared directly — nothing is sent to our servers during export.
7 Children's Privacy

Habit Flow is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at keshavkatariya2004@gmail.com.

8 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. When you request account deletion, your data is permanently and automatically deleted upon your next sign-in attempt after a 30-day period. During this 30-day window, you may cancel the deletion by signing back in.

If you never sign in again after requesting deletion, your data will be retained until you do, or until we perform periodic manual cleanups of inactive accounts. To request immediate deletion, contact us at keshavkatariya2004@gmail.com.

We will not retain your data beyond what is required for legal or legitimate business purposes.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by email. Your continued use of Habit Flow after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
